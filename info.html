Key Info

  • Where is PennFest?

    PennFest is nestled amongst the rolling valleys of South Wales. To be exact - Cymmer, Port Talbot, SA13 3HG. Plug us into your satnav and you should find us with no trouble. There will also be plenty of signs along the way, as soon as you leave the motorway.

  • How long is PennFest?

    The main portion of PennFest will kick off on the Friday morning at 9am, running through until our closing ceremony on Sunday at around 11pm. The campsite however will be open for a full 24hours either side of these times, so feel free to head in a little early and sleep in a little late if needed!

  • What can I bring to PennFest?

    The usual festival fare is fine by us. Feel free to bring your own alcohol into the campsite, however you will not be able to take this into the festival area itself. Also be aware of searches when both entering and exiting the festival grounds. Anything illegal will be confiscated and the on-site authorities informed.

  • How can I pay at PennFest?

    PennFest is a completely cash-free event. Contactless is available across the site and we accept all the usual payment methods.

  • Medical services at PennFest?

    Medical services are available at all times, with first-aid tents dotted around both the event and camping areas, all open 24-hours a day.
